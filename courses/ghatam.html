About Us

Introduction

Sri Veenavani School of Music has successfully completed 41 years as a prestigious & renowned institution. We have made tremendous achievements by imparting music education to over 15,000 students till date in the age group of 4 to 70 years. About 14 different instruments and vocals in both Carnatic and Hindustani styles, as well as light music and playback singing are taught here.

Sri Veenavani Music School is a premier music school in South Bangalore and is making landmark achievements in the field of music.

Guinness Record Holders For Largest Keyboard Ensemble

Sri Veena Vani Music School is a Premier Carnatic Classical Music School in South Bangalore, having its branches also in Virginia, USA and Sydney, Australia. Having made landmark of achievements in the field of music, we take pride in having imparted music education to over 15,000 students till date from age groups varying 4yrs to 70yrs and teaching more than 14 different instruments and vocals in both Carnatic, Hindustani Styles, Light Music and Playback Singing, the institution takes pride in birthing passions and perseverance for all aspiring students and enabling a platform in all musical facets.

Carnatic classical music which has grown to the heights of Himalayas has been adapted by various kinds of western musical instruments and efforts are still being made in adapting it. To continue this wonderful heritage and to make a mark to its existing credibility as we celebrate our 40th Year anniversary we wish to add another milestone in attempting a Guinness book of world record for the world’s largest keyboard ensemble in playing the National Anthem, National song and a carnatic raga on a western instrument towards a social cause.

Previous Activities

Sri Veenavani School of Music has conducted many stage shows which have been extensively covered by all major newspapers. For four years, the talent-search show, ‘Pratibhanveshane’ has been attended by about 3000-4000 people. We have also conducted a show where the proceeds have been used to purchase books for the less-fortunate students among us. Our program called Taare Zameen Par, has attracted about 5000 people, and all proceeds in the form of ticket sales have gone to charity. This is apart from the annual stage show conducted by our school which showcases the talents of all our students.

The School's History

This school was started by Vidwan Sri Sampath Kumar Sharma, and was mentored by him for 26 years. He was the student of Mysore Aasthana Vidwan Sri Somashekhar Rajamani.

Sri Gireesh Kumar

Sri Gireesh Kumar, who is the student of Sri Sampath Kumar Sharma, has been nurturing this prominent institution for the past 8 years. He is proficient in 8 instruments and 4 types of vocals. He started his education in music at the age of 6 years & emerged as the sensational winning director of a State-level Drama Competition at the age of 19. He has successfully directed 23 dramas in Kannada and 1 in Hindi. He has also composed splendid music for over 450 stage plays and more than 100 regional language feature films and short films.
